Sights to See: Mercat S'Josep La Bouqueria

When in Barcelona, one should stop by the Mercat La Bouqueria to browse fresh, locally produced foods or buy a souvenir.

The Basílica de la Sagrada Família

The Basílica de la Sagrada Família, also known as the Sagrada Família, is a large unfinished Roman Catholic minor basilica in Barcelona, Catalonia and is a beautiful piece of architecture one must take the opportunity to gaze at when in Barcelona.

Park Guell

Park Guell is a famous view that one might already be familiar with. Perched in Barcelona's Horta-Guinardó neighbourhood, this park boasts breathtaking sights of the whole city.

The Magic Fountain of Montjuïc

This fountain located at the head of Avinguda Maria Cristina in the Montjuïc neighborhood of Barcelona, Catalonia, Spain. Many tourists and locals like will watch a fountain performance, in which the fountain shoots water and changes color in synchronization to music.
